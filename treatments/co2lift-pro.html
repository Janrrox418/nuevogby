CO2LIFT PRO

At Gbyskincare, we offer CO2LIFT PRO — an advanced carbon dioxide rejuvenation that pairs CO2 therapy with potent serums to increase oxygenation, stimulate circulation, and accelerate skin renewal. The result is improved texture, softer fine lines, and deep hydration for a radiant, plumper complexion.

What Is CO2LIFT PRO?

It’s a customized treatment that uses controlled CO2 application together with active topicals to:

  • Enhance oxygen delivery to the skin
  • Improve skin tone and texture
  • Reduce the appearance of fine lines and immediately boost glow
  • Require minimal downtime — ideal for a lunchtime refresh

Benefits You Can Expect

After a session or a short series, many clients notice:

  • A brighter, more even complexion
  • Smoother texture and reduced pores
  • Improved hydration and plumper skin
  • Reduced appearance of fine lines

Who Is It For?

CO2LIFT PRO is appropriate for clients seeking skin renewal, improved tone/texture, and a quick rejuvenating treatment with low downtime.

Gentle Yet Effective

Designed for visible results without harsh downtime, most clients experience only mild warmth or redness that fades quickly.

Your Glow Awaits

We personalize every CO2LIFT PRO based on skin goals. Whether smoothing texture, brightening tone, or refreshing your complexion, this treatment can help you look and feel more confident.

FAQ: CO2LIFT PRO

Answers to common questions about CO2LIFT PRO

CO2LIFT PRO uses controlled carbon dioxide application to increase local blood flow and oxygen delivery. When combined with active serums, it accelerates skin renewal, boosts collagen, and improves hydration and radiance.

Most clients experience a mild warming or tingling sensation. We use gentle protocols and comfort measures to keep you relaxed during the session.

Many clients see noticeable improvement after one session. For more significant or long-lasting results, a series of 3–6 treatments spaced 2–4 weeks apart is often recommended.

Side effects are typically mild and temporary — slight redness or warmth that subsides within a few hours. There is usually no downtime, and many clients return to normal activities immediately.
