Astrodome Facial

Korean Glass Skin Facial

The Astrodome Facial is a state-of-the-art treatment that combines Oxygen Therapy, LED Light Therapy, and Anion Mist to achieve the coveted "Glass Skin" look. This Korean protocol deeply repairs cells, boosts immunity, and hydrates at a cellular level.

How it works: The Astrodome mask creates a hyperbaric-like environment, infusing pure oxygen and anions (vitamins from the air) while simultaneous LED lights (Red, Blue, Violet) target specific skin concerns.

  • Glass Skin Effect: Instantly brighter, translucent skin.
  • Deep Healing: Repairs damaged skin barrier and calms inflammation.
  • Anion Therapy: Neutralizes free radicals and rebalances skin pH.

Fire & Ice Facial

The Red Carpet Facial

Designed by iS Clinical, this legendary facial is designed to rapidly and safely resurface the skin with zero downtime. It bridges the gap between a mild facial and an invasive peel.

The Protocol:

  • Fire: An intensive resurfacing masque with Glycolic Acid, Retinol, and Niacinamide heats up the skin to exfoliate and stimulate cell turnover.
  • Ice: A rejuvenating masque with Hyaluronic Acid, Aloe Vera, and Green Tea cools and soothes, providing deep hydration.

The result is a glowing, refined, and photo-ready complexion, making it the favorite choice for celebrities before big events.
