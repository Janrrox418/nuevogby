Glo2facial by Geneo

✨ What it is: A next-generation, medical-grade facial that cleanses, exfoliates, hydrates, and revitalizes the skin in one session.

🔬 How it works: It combines three core steps:

Oxfoliation – Oxygenating exfoliation with OxyPods + Primer Gel.
Lite Ultrasound (LUX) – Micro-vibrations that smooth skin and enhance absorption.
Lymphatic Massage – Detoxifies, reduces puffiness, and sculpts contours.

💧 Benefits:

  • Brighter, more even skin tone
  • Smoother texture & reduced fine lines
  • Calms breakouts & hydrates deeply
  • Zero downtime, instant results

Light Therapy

✨ What it is: A non-invasive treatment that uses specific wavelengths of light to target skin concerns and stimulate healing.

🔬 How it works:

Red light – Boosts collagen, reduces inflammation.
Blue light – Kills acne bacteria, calms breakouts.
Yellow light – Improves circulation, reduces redness.
Near-infrared – Deep penetration, repairs tissue.

💧 Benefits:

  • Reduces fine lines & wrinkles
  • Clears acne & prevents breakouts
  • Calms redness, rosacea & irritation
  • Speeds up wound healing
  • Improves overall skin tone

Vasculyse

🩸 What it is: Non-invasive treatment using radiofrequency thermocoagulation to eliminate small vascular & skin irregularities.

🔬 How it works:

A fine probe delivers high-frequency current to coagulate blood in tiny vessels.
The body reabsorbs treated tissue, regenerating healthy skin.
Only touches the skin surface — no piercing.

✨ Common conditions treated:

  • Spider veins
  • Broken capillaries
  • Cherry angiomas
  • Skin tags
  • Milia
  • Cholesterol deposits
