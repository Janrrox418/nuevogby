The Skin Ritual Membership Terms

Membership Commitment

The Skin Ritual Membership requires a 3-month initial commitment. Following this term, it automatically renews on a month-to-month basis until canceled. This ensures consistency for optimal results.

Billing & Payments

Fees are billed monthly on your enrollment date. Fees are non-refundable. If a payment is declined, you have 10 days to update it before benefits are paused.

Cancellations

Must be submitted in writing with 30 days’ notice prior to your next billing date. Any unused treatment credits must be used before the membership ends.

Rollover & Transfers

Rollover: Unused credits roll over for up to 90 days.
Transfers: Credits may be transferred to friends/family (treatment only; excludes member pricing/exclusive benefits).

Membership Pause

Members may request one pause per 12-month period, for up to 2 months total. Billing and benefits are suspended during this time.

Program Adjustments

GbySkincare reserves the right to update pricing, benefits, or terms with 30 days’ written notice. All services are subject to professional assessment.

Acknowledgment

By enrolling in The Skin Ritual Membership, you acknowledge that you have read and agree to these terms. You authorize GbySkincare to charge your payment method on a recurring monthly basis.
